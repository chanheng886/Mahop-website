about us

History

Owner and Siem Reap restaurateur Sothea Seng was looking
for a unique location for his new venture, and when he came
across this traditional Khmer house just off Charles de Gaulle,
he knew he had found the perfect spot. The elegant property
formerly belonged to a governor of Angkor Thom’s Peak
Sneng district.

Beautifully decorated in the Khmer style, with original
features such as carved wooden roof details and blinds, the
house boasts an airy wooden upstairs section as well as a
contemporary ground floor area that has been transformed
into the main dining room, bar, and VIP air-conditioned
room. The house has been carefully restored in keeping with
all its original design features, including an attractive leaf
roof sourced from the Kulen Mountain area.

chef

A young and dynamic chef and also the restaurant’s owner,
Sothea has always had a passion for food. With over ten
years’ experience both locally and in luxury hotels in Dubai
and the Caribbean, Sothea has gradually developed his unique
cooking style.

His culinary flair has led him to open some of Siem Reap’s top
restaurants, including Nest Angkor Café, Hot Stone Café, and
Palate Angkor. He also helped develop the Italian restaurant
Contro in Phnom Penh and was formerly the head chef at The
Plantation restaurant.

Sothea loves nothing more than to create original dishes,
taking inspiration from organic produce and using the best
local suppliers to ensure you have an exceptional dining
experience every time.
